Healthy Recipes

Protein Smoothie Bowl

A healthy breakfast packed with protein, fruits and nutrients for energy.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Banana
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 1 Scoop Protein Powder
  • Mixed Berries
  • Oats

Recipe:

Blend banana, milk and protein powder. Pour into a bowl and top with berries, oats and nuts. Serve chilled.

Grilled Chicken Salad

High-protein salad perfect for lunch and muscle recovery.

Ingredients:

  • Grilled Chicken
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olive Oil

Recipe:

Grill the chicken with light seasoning. Mix vegetables in a bowl and place sliced chicken on top. Add olive oil dressing.

Quinoa Buddha Bowl

A complete healthy meal with grains, vegetables and healthy fats.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Quinoa
  • Avocado
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Boiled Chickpeas

Recipe:

Add quinoa to a bowl and top with vegetables, avocado and chickpeas. Drizzle with lemon dressing.
